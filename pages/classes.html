Recreational Team1 - Fall Classes Thursdays

First Year Students, 4 year old and up

Classes run from September 14th through November 2nd (8 classes - $115)

Thursday 5:00 - 6:00 pm

During this class we will show children how to handle various apparatuses, such as a hoops, ropes, ribbons, and balls. The program would be a basic introduction to the wonderful and fun sport of rhythmic gymnastics, which teaches grace and rhythm while strengthening core muscles. Please wear clothes that are easy to move in, such as leotard, leggings and shorts (please, black and white colors only) Gymnasts practice with socks or barefoot.

Performance Team

8 classes a month $95

Sunday 1:30 - 3:00 pm
Tuesday 5:00 - 6:30 pm

Performance team will meet twice a week and will take part in all performances and club competitions. We currently offer a wide variety of classes that suit the needs of between the ages of 4 to 15. Our goal is to help young women improve their physical fitness and develop a strong mental focus while providing a valuable outlet for personal growth. We create an environment that leads to opportunities anywhere from competitions across the country to a fun seasonal performance at our gym.


*note that there is a $54 fee per year for a USA Rythmic Gymnastics membership for Performance and Competitive Teams
